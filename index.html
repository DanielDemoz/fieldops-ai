Platform Features

Intelligent Scheduling

Automated job scheduling with route optimization to minimize travel time and maximize daily job completion. Assigns technicians based on skills, location, and availability.

Time & Attendance Tracking

GPS-verified time tracking with automated anomaly detection. Ensures accurate labor costing and prevents time theft while providing real-time technician location.

Inventory Management

Track parts and materials usage across jobs. Automated reorder alerts prevent stockouts. Link inventory to specific work orders for accurate job costing.

Automated Invoicing

Generate professional invoices automatically from completed work orders. Includes labor hours, materials costs, and tax calculations. Export to PDF for immediate sending.

Cash Flow Analytics

Forecast cash flow with predictive analytics. Track revenue trends, payment status, and predict future cash gaps. Get alerts before cash flow becomes critical.

Performance Metrics

Comprehensive KPIs including profit per job, technician utilization, travel time metrics, and material cost variance. Make data-driven decisions with confidence.
